Tao Kwon Do is a form of Martial arts designed for self defense.
The meaning of Tae Kwon Do is Korean and the word Tae means jumping and flying, to kick or smash with the foot.
Kwon denotes the fist, to punch or destroy with the hand or fist and Do means the art or way.
Tae Kwon Do was started by retired Korean army General Cho Hong Li.
The beggining of Tae Kwon Do goes back thousands of years and was originally used for unarmed combat and self defense.
practice Tae Kwon Do to become physically strong and be active and to have self control.
